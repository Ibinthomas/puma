PRODUCT STORY

Straight from the archives, it’s the PUMA Palermo. This classic terrace shoe is revived today with its signature T-toe construction, plus extra-bold colour blocking and plush materials. Its design builds on PUMA’s heritage within football, while appealing to today’s sneakerheads and terrace fashion mavens.
